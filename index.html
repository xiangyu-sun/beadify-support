Turn photos into bead art.

📷 Any source

Import from your photo library, take a photo with your camera, or pick from 200+ emoji across 8 categories.

🎨 Perceptual color matching

Every pixel snaps to the nearest real bead color using Oklab — a perceptually uniform color space designed for exactly this.

🔢 Exact bead counts

Color legend shows how many of each bead you need. Bead codes appear inside cells at high zoom so you never lose your place.

🔍 Zoom & pan

Pinch to zoom, pan freely. Rulers and major gridlines every 5 cells. Tap any cell to identify the bead color and code.

〰️ Dithering

Floyd-Steinberg or Bayer ordered dithering for smooth gradients and subtle color transitions.

☁️ iCloud sync

Patterns sync automatically across your devices via iCloud. Your data stays yours — no server, no account.
